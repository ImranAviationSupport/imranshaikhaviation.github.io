# Services

Neo Cubz Aerospace & Engineering provides a wide range of aviation services designed to support safe, compliant, and efficient operations.

---

## Aviation Consulting
Strategic and technical advisory for operators, training organisations, and emerging aviation businesses.  
Areas include operational readiness, compliance, maintenance strategy, and organisational development.

---

## LAME Contracting (Aeroplane & Powerplant)
Professional maintenance support including:
- Line maintenance  
- Defect rectification  
- Scheduled/unscheduled maintenance  
- Mobile maintenance capability  

---

## Airworthiness & Engineering
Support for operators and CAMOs including:
- Technical investigations  
- Reliability and maintenance optimisation  
- Documentation and engineering support  
- Airworthiness reviews and assessments  

---

## Safety & Quality Assurance
Strengthening organisational safety and compliance through:
- Internal audits  
- SMS support  
- Quality assurance reviews  
- Safety investigations  

---

## Targeted Training
Customised training for:
- Maintenance personnel  
- Human factors  
- Safety and quality  
- Technical upskilling
