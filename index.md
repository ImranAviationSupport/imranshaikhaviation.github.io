# Neo Cubz Aerospace & Engineering  
### Trading under Neo Cubz Ltd  
#### Aviation Consulting • LAME Contracting • Airworthiness • Safety & Quality • Targeted Training

Neo Cubz Aerospace & Engineering provides specialist aviation consulting and technical services across New Zealand and internationally. With extensive experience in military and civil aviation environments, we support operators, CAMOs, and training organisations with practical, safety‑driven engineering solutions.

Our approach combines technical depth, regulatory understanding, and values‑based leadership to deliver outcomes that strengthen safety, reliability, and operational performance.
