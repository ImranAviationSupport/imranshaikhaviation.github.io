# About Neo Cubz Aerospace & Engineering

Neo Cubz Aerospace & Engineering (trading under Neo Cubz Ltd) is an aviation consultancy based in Aotearoa New Zealand, providing engineering, airworthiness, and safety services to operators, training organisations, and international partners.

Founded by Imran Shaikh, an experienced aviation engineer and LAME (Aeroplane & Powerplant), the consultancy brings together technical expertise, regulatory knowledge, and values‑driven leadership.

---

## Professional Background

Imran has extensive experience across:
- RNZAF engineering and maintenance operations  
- Civil aviation maintenance and airworthiness  
- Safety and quality assurance  
- Technical documentation and compliance  
- Training and workforce development  

He holds:
- Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) – Mechanical  
- Postgraduate Certificate in Business (Strategic Management)  
- ISO 9001 Lead Auditor  
- Lean Six Sigma  
- Aeroplane & Powerplant group ratings

## Our Values

Our work is grounded in Māori principles that guide how we engage and deliver:

- **Manaakitanga** – Respect, care, and professionalism  
- **Whanaungatanga** – Strong relationships and collaboration  
- **Kaitiakitanga** – Responsibility, safety, and stewardship  

These values shape our approach to engineering, safety, and leadership.
